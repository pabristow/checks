Modulus 11 algorithm

The modulus 11 algorithm use a modulus of 11, so we have 11 possible check digits. The ten first characters are the figures from 0 to 9, the eleventh is a special character choose by the designer of the number. It is typically 'X' or 'x'. The weight of a digit is related to its position. The weight of the first character is equal to the total length of the number (with the check digit included). The weight decreases by one for the second position, one again for the third, etc.
